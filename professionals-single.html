Martha O’Hara Interiors designs beautiful spaces with the goal of connecting life and style. Whether you are building a new home or updating your furniture, we offer functionality, style, and value. Our award-winning designers love to work in a variety of styles and range of aesthetics to create interiors that allow you to be yourself, freely and comfortably. With locations in Minnesota and Texas, we work with homeowners and building professionals from coast to coast.
